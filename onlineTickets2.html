Purchase Tickets Online

  • No physical tickets will be mailed to you..

  • How this works... your name and ticket quantity will be on a will-call list at the performance. Simply arrive at the performance and inform the folks at the ticket desk that you have purchased your ticket(s) online. They will find your name on the will-call list.
  • There are THREE links below - one link for each performance. Click the link corresponding to the performance that you wish to attend. You will be taken to Zeffy's website - follow their directions to purchase your tickets. Zeffy does not charge a service fee for tickets (to you or to the Messiah Singalong), but they will ask you if you'd like to leave a tip to cover their transaction costs. If you prefer, you can purchase your advance tickets in person at our retail location. Note that our retail location accepts cash or check only, whereas you can use a credit or debit card, PayPal, or GooglePay when purchasing tickets online.
  • See our policy regarding tickets for children and purchase tickets as appropriate for any children attending a performance with you.
  • Once you have completed your purchase of online tickets, look for a purchase confirmation arriving in your email INBOX in just a few minutes (check your SPAM folder if the email doesn't show up in your INBOX within a few minutes)
