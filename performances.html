The dates and times for the 2023 Messiah Sing-Along are:

  • Saturday, December 16th, 2023 @ 5:00pm
  • Sunday, December 17th, 2023 @ 2:00pm

All performances occur at St. John's Episcopal Church, 14th and Pine, in downtown Boulder (see map below). St John's Episcopal is conveniently located one block north of the Pearl Street pedestrian mall which offers hundreds of shops and restaurants and delightful street entertainers. A number of parking garages are available in downtown Boulder (garages are usually free on Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays), as well as on-street parking.

Traditionally, the Sunday @ 2pm matinee is the performance most likely to sell out, while the Sunday @ 7pm performance almost never sells out and is the most relaxed performance of the three.

Tickets

Tickets can be purchased

Retail outlets
