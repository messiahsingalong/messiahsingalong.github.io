The Boulder Messiah Chorale and Orchestra has been staging community sing-alongs of the Messiah since 1983. Putting on these performances is an enormous undertaking, accomplished in part via the efforts of many volunteers who are happy and eager to lend their labor and talent to help keep this community tradition alive. But even with an army of happy, eager volunteers, some things still cost money: professional musicians must be paid, newspaper advertisements must be purchased, performance and practice venues must be rented, programs must be printed, ......

Income from ticket sales accounts for only a portion of the production costs. We rely on community donations to help fill in the gaps. Your donation of any amount would be most appreciated and would help ensure that this cherished community tradition continues well into the future. The Boulder Messiah Chorale and Orchestra is a 501(c)(3) non-profit musical organization, which means that your donation is eligible for a tax deduction (subject to your own personal tax situation).

We are happy to accept your donation in any of the following ways:

  • At any time... personal checks made out to Boulder Messiah Chorale and Orchestra, can be mailed to us. Please click here to contact us via e-mail for the mailing address.
  • When buying your tickets online via credit card, you may include an optional donation at the time of purchase.
  • At performances. Personal checks, made out to Boulder Messiah Chorale and Orchestra, can be given to the staff at the ticket desk.
